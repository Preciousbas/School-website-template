APPLICATION PROCESS

All children to be admitted shall have their admission forms completed by their parents/guardians. Children admitted are usually between the ages of two and five (2 – 5 year olds) for the Pre-school and Reception classes, while the ages for the Primary classes are between five and six years (5 – 6 year olds). The selection process depends largely on scores obtained from I.Q. tests and other types of tests/interviews administered by the school. Offer of admission is however on a first come, first served basis.

The admission form can be gotten from the school premises. Parents are advised to fill the admission forms correctly. This is to ensure that proper record of information about the applicant is obtained. Those who pass the test and interviews consequently get an offer of admission.
